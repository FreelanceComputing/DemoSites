How do I place an order?

From the product page, select the relevant shipping location and size. These will determine whether or not the item is in stock.
If it isn't, the 'Add to bag' button will not be active and you will not be able to add this item to your shopping bag. If the item is available, you will be able to add it to your shopping bag and proceed to checkout.
 

How do I order a product from Cameroon?

This option is not yet available via the website as we are looking to introduce mobile money payments for orders from Cameroon. Please contact doualatees@gmail.com if you want to place an order from Cameroon.
 

How long does it take to process an order?

Once your order has been placed, it will be processed within 2 business days. Orders placed on Fridays - Sundays will be processed on Monday and Tuesday. Orders placed on a UK or AUS public holiday will be processed on the following 2 business days.
 

How do I order a personalised T-shirt?

Please send an email to doualatees@gmail.com with a description of what your requirements are. We will respond to you within 2 business days with a personalised quote and delivery estimate.

Which cards do you accept?

We accept debit and credit cards from the following providers: Visa, Mastercard and American Express.
 

Do you accept PayPal?

Not via the website. Please send an email to doualatees@gmail.com if you would prefer to make a payment via PayPal. Note that a 2% surcharge will be applied.
 

Are my card details secure?

Yes. We take care not to store any payment information on our website. These details are sent directly to our payment provider for processing. This website also has a valid and up to date SSL certificate for improved security.
 

I am based in Cameroon. How do I pay for an item?

Payments in Cameroon are accepted via mobile money. Please contact doualatees@gmail.com for further information. Note that your mobile money payment should include the transaction fee.

Where do you ship to?

We ship to America, Australia and Europe.
 

Do you ship to Cameroon?

No. Shipping is not available in Cameroon. All orders received from Cameroon need to be collected in person from our designated stockists. The following will be required to collect your item:
 
  • Order confirmation message.
  • Valid Cameroon national identity card.
 

Do you ship to other African countries?

No we don't.

How do I return an item?

Please send us an email with your order confirmation number for the item you wish to return. We will then provide you with a returns address depending on where your item was shipped from.
 

Will I get a full refund?

In order to get a full product refund, the returned item must be unused and undamaged with no stains such as from dirt, sweat, makeup etc. Unfortunately shipping charges are non-refundable.
 

What happens if receive a different or damaged product?

If we send you the wrong item or you receive a damaged item, please notify us within 24 hours of the item being received so that we can exchange the item for you.

Washing

All items can be machine washed on cold cycle only. Accessories like ball caps should be hand washed.
 

Ironing

Tops, hoodies and tees should be ironed on the reverse side using a cool setting. Accessories should not be ironed.
 

Dry Cleaning

Do not dry clean.
